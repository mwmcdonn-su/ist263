About Copenhagen:

The Danes are known has the happiest people in the world. This couldn’t be truer, from living life extremely sustainability friendly, exciting restaurants to eat, incredible architecture, and lots of royal history: Copenhagen is truly the happiest place on earth. From free college tuition, extensive employee vacation says, and shorter weekdays, there is truly nothing not to like about this city. New York City is known as the city that never sleeps, but Copenhageners are known to bike up to 1.44 million kilometers daily, which is an estimated 894,774 miles a day. Copenhagen is located right on the coastal islands of Zealand and Amager. Being the largest city in Copenhagen, there is always so much to offer. It truly is the happiest city in the world for a multitude of reasons.

Things To Do:

1) Visit the Amalienborg Palace, where the Danish Royal Family lives

2)Enjoy the magic of the Tivoli Gardens, one of the oldest theme parks in the word

3)Take a picture of The Little Mermaid sculpture, inspired by Hans Christian Andersen’s famous fairy tale

4)Walk up The Round Tower, to get the more iconic view of Copenhagen

5)Stroll down the 17th-century waterfront canal on Nyhavn

6)Grab a Danish hot dog while walking the Streets of Copenhagen

7)Rent a bike and bike around the city to see the most amazing views

8)Must try an open sandwich, which is a tradition to the Danish people

9)Tour the Christiansburg Palace, where you can experience centuries of royal history

10) Take pictures at The Botanical Gardens and make sure to visit the butterfly house
