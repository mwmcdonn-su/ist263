About Copenhagen

The Danes are known has the happiest people in the world and this could not be more true. From living life extremely sustainability friendly, exciting restaurants to eat, incredible architecture, and lots of royal history: Copenhagen is truly the happiest place on earth. Denmark offers free college tuition, extensive employee vacation says, and shorter weekdays, there is truly nothing not to like about it. New York City is known as the city that never sleeps, but Danes are known to bike up to 1.44 million kilometers daily, which is an estimated 894,774 miles a day. Copenhagen is located right on the coastal islands of Zealand and Amager, which makes it easy to travel where-ever. Being the largest city in Copenhagen, there is always so much to offer. If you have the chance to visit, you totally should!
